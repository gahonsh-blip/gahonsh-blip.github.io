Pricing Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to help you understand our operational onboarding and payment loops.

What's included in revisions?
Revisions include text changes, column/row restructuring, formula adjustments, and layout fine-tuning based on the initial project scope sheet.
How do payments work?
We operate on a milestone-driven system. Projects are divided into clear deliverables, and payments are securely cleared after each stage is verified.
Can pricing change during a project?
No. Once a fixed quote or milestone budget is officially agreed upon, the price remains locked unless completely new requirements are added outside the initial brief.
Do you offer custom multi-page packages?
Absolutely. If your business workflows require a mixed setup (e.g., combining custom Excel scripts with an interactive PDF profile), we can draft a tailored quote sheet.
